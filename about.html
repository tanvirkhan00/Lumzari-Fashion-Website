How It Began

A Mother's Dream,
A Community's Need

✦

Lumzari was born from a simple wish — a mother searching for beautiful, modest clothing for her daughter that didn't compromise on either style or Islamic values. Finding very little in the market, she began stitching dreams into fabric.

Today, we serve hundreds of families across Bangladesh, offering carefully crafted dresses, abayas, and hijabs that allow little girls to celebrate their identity with grace. Every piece is designed with love, tested for comfort, and finished to boutique standard.

We believe that when a child wears something beautiful and meaningful, it nurtures their confidence, their faith, and their sense of self — from the very first years of their life.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Every stitch, every choice, every design is guided by principles we hold close to our hearts.

🌸
Comfort First

Children move, play, and explore. Our fabrics are soft, breathable, and tested to ensure all-day comfort without irritation — because happy kids wear comfortable clothes.

✨
Timeless Elegance

We draw inspiration from the beauty of Islamic art and garden motifs — creating garments that feel both timeless and modern, suitable for everyday wear and special occasions.

🕌
Islamic Values

Modesty is not a restriction — it's a gift. We design every piece with the intention of making girls feel proud of their identity, their deen, and their uniqueness.

💚
Ethical Production

All our clothing is produced locally in Dhaka with fair wages and safe working conditions. We support artisan embroiderers and local fabric craftspeople.

🎁
Premium Gifting

Our packaging is designed to feel like opening a gift — because every child deserves to feel special. Perfect for Eid, birthdays, and Aqiqah celebrations.

🤝
Community

We're more than a store — we're a community of mothers, sisters, and families who care about raising the next generation with beauty, pride, and faith.

Our Journey

From Dream
to Reality

A small home studio, a loving community, and a vision for modest beauty — this is how Lumzari grew.

22
2022 — The Beginning
A Dream Takes Shape

Lumzari launched from a small home studio in Dhaka, stitching the first 10 modest dresses by hand and sharing them with family and friends who instantly fell in love.

23
2023 — First Eid Collection
Sold Out in Three Days

Our debut Eid collection sold out in just 3 days. We expanded our range to include abayas and hijabs, partnering with local artisans for signature embroidery work.

24
2024 — Going Digital
Reaching Families Nationwide

We launched our online store, reaching families in Chittagong, Sylhet, Rajshahi and beyond. Over 100 five-star reviews poured in from delighted parents across Bangladesh.

25
2025 & Beyond
Growing the Family

Expanding to serve mothers and sisters too. A complete family modest fashion line is in development — stay tuned for our women's collection launch soon.
